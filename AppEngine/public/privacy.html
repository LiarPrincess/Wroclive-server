Privacy Policy

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on Wroclive website and our apps for iPhone and any other devices and platforms.

The app itself collects no information about you and refrains from logging how it is used. Wroclive will log requests to the server, the document requested and some basic technical information (such as your IP address).

Wroclive app asks for permission to access your current location, but this information is never transmitted to Wroclive servers. The app contains no crash reporting or analytics frameworks.

Third-party links and content

Wroclive displays links to third-party sites (such as github.com). These have their own independent privacy policies, and we have no responsibility or liability for their content or activities.
